Razer expands range of PlayStation®5 products with new Kaira Pro and Kaira headsets

Completing their PlayStation®5 headset line-up, Razer launches a series of wireless headsets for console fans, including the first ever wireless PS5™ headset powered by Razer™ HyperSense intelligent haptics technology

IRVINE, Calif. – Razer™, the leading global lifestyle brand for gamers (Hong Kong Stock Code: 1337), today announced they are expanding their rapidly growing console line-up with a series of new wireless headsets and controller charging stands for the PlayStation®5. Through the black and white motifs and signature blue accents, the new Kaira headsets are designed to match the contemporary and modern style of the PlayStation®5 console and DualSense™ Wireless Controller.

Also launching today is the Razer Quick Charging Stand in a range of matching colors to create the perfect pairing to any gamer’s PS5™ DualSense™ Wireless Controller.

The Razer Kaira Pro Dual Wireless Headset with Haptics unleashes true console immersion. It is Razer’s first wireless headset made for PlayStation®5 that is powered by Razer™ HyperSense, an intelligent haptics technology that delivers rich, lifelike touch sensory feedback based on audio cues, allowing gamers to submerge their senses in full tactile gameplay.

The Razer Kaira Pro is equipped with the tried-and-true Razer™ TriForce Titanium 50mm Drivers for absolute clarity while being light enough to ensure comfort over long periods of use.

Enhancing in-game communications through its detachable Razer™ HyperClear Supercardioid Mic, the Razer Kaira Pro focuses on a tight vocal recognition pattern while suppressing unwanted background noise to achieve greater vocal clarity.

The wireless headset also offers a built-in microphone for mobile usage, providing users the options to connect to their mobile devices through Bluetooth as well as the PS5™, PS4™, PC, and other devices that have a USB-C or USB-A port via an included 2.4 GHz USB-C Razer™ HyperSpeed Dongle (USB-A adapter included).

Completing the PlayStation®5 headset line-up is the Razer Kaira Dual Wireless Headset. Whether on console or mobile, gamers can enjoy unrivalled convenience when gaming with Razer™ SmartSwitch, which allows for seamless switching between 2.4 GHz and Bluetooth devices.

Also being announced today are the Razer Quick Charging Stands. The stands were thoughtfully designed in a range of matching colors to suit the galaxy-inspired PS5™ DualSense™ Wireless Controllers.

The Razer Quick Charging Stand rapidly charges controllers to reach prime power levels in under 3 hours and ensures minimal gaming downtime for PS5™ users. It is available in White, Black and Red colors to achieve a seamless look.
